Coronavirus what it is?

Coronaviruses are a type of virus. There are many different kinds, and some cause disease. A coronavirus identified in 2019, SARS-CoV-2, has caused a pandemic of respiratory illness, called COVID-19.

How to Protect Yourself

Protect yourself and others around you by knowing the facts and taking appropriate precautions. Follow advice provided by your local health authority.

Wear Mask

Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible.

Wash Your Hands

Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.

Stay at Home

Stay home if you feel unwell.Keeping distance from others is especially important for people who are at higher risk of getting very sick.

Social Distancing

Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.

Get Vaccinated

Authorized COVID-19 vaccines can help protect you from COVID-19. You should get a COVID-19 vaccine when it is available to you.
